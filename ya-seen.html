1

يٰسٓ ۚ‏ ١۝

Yaa-Seeen

-Ya, Seen.

Tafsir

2

وَالۡقُرۡاٰنِ الۡحَكِيۡمِ ۙ‏ ٢۝

Wal-Qur-aanil-Hakeem

-By the Quran, rich in wisdom!

Tafsir

3

اِنَّكَ لَمِنَ الۡمُرۡسَلِيۡنَۙ‏ ٣۝

Innaka laminal mursaleen

-You ˹O Prophet˺ are truly one of the messengers

Tafsir

4

عَلٰى صِرَاطٍ مُّسۡتَقِيۡمٍؕ‏ ٤۝

'Alaa Siraatim Mustaqeem

-On a straight path.

Tafsir

5

تَنۡزِيۡلَ الۡعَزِيۡزِ الرَّحِيۡمِ ۙ‏ ٥۝

Tanzeelal 'Azeezir Raheem

-[This is] a revelation of the Exalted in Might, the Merciful,

Tafsir

6

لِتُنۡذِرَ قَوۡمًا مَّاۤ اُنۡذِرَ اٰبَآؤُهُمۡ فَهُمۡ غٰفِلُوۡنَ‏ ٦۝

Litunzira qawmam maaa unzira aabaaa'uhum fahum ghaafiloon

-That you may warn a people whose forefathers were not warned, so they are unaware.

Tafsir

7

لَقَدۡ حَقَّ الۡقَوۡلُ عَلٰٓى اَكۡثَرِهِمۡ فَهُمۡ لَا يُؤۡمِنُوۡنَ‏ ٧۝

Laqad haqqal qawlu 'alaaa aksarihim fahum laa yu'minoon

-Already the word has come into effect upon most of them, so they do not believe.

Tafsir

8

اِنَّا جَعَلۡنَا فِىۡۤ اَعۡنَاقِهِمۡ اَغۡلٰلًا فَهِىَ اِلَى الۡاَ ذۡقَانِ فَهُمۡ مُّقۡمَحُوۡنَ‏ ٨۝

Innaa ja'alnaa feee a'naaqihim aghlaalan fahiya ilal azqaani fahum muqmahoon

-Indeed, We have put shackles on their necks, and they are to their chins, so they are with heads [kept] aloft.

Tafsir

9

وَجَعَلۡنَا مِنۡۢ بَيۡنِ اَيۡدِيۡهِمۡ سَدًّا وَّمِنۡ خَلۡفِهِمۡ سَدًّا فَاَغۡشَيۡنٰهُمۡ فَهُمۡ لَا يُبۡصِرُوۡنَ‏ ٩۝

Wa ja'alnaa mim baini aydeehim saddanw-wa min khalfihim saddan fa aghshai naahum fahum laa yubsiroon

-And We have put before them a barrier and behind them a barrier and covered them, so they do not see.

Tafsir

10

وَسَوَآءٌ عَلَيۡهِمۡ ءَاَنۡذَرۡتَهُمۡ اَمۡ لَمۡ تُنۡذِرۡهُمۡ لَا يُؤۡمِنُوۡنَ‏ ﻿﻿ ٠١۝

Wa sawaaa'un 'alaihim 'a-anzartahum am lam tunzirhum laa yu'minoon

-And it is all the same for them whether you warn them or do not warn them - they will not believe.

Tafsir

11

اِنَّمَا تُنۡذِرُ مَنِ اتَّبَعَ الذِّكۡرَ وَخَشِىَ الرَّحۡمٰنَ بِالۡغَيۡبِۚ فَبَشِّرۡهُ بِمَغۡفِرَةٍ وَّاَجۡرٍ كَرِيۡمٍ‏ ١١۝

Innamaa tunziru manit taba 'az-Zikra wa khashiyar Rahmaana bilghaib, fabashshirhu bimaghfiratinw-wa ajrin kareem

-You can only warn one who follows the message and fears the Most Merciful unseen. So give him good tidings of forgiveness and noble reward.

Tafsir

12

اِنَّا نَحۡنُ نُحۡىِ الۡمَوۡتٰى وَنَكۡتُبُ مَا قَدَّمُوۡا وَاٰثَارَهُمۡؕ وَكُلَّ شَىۡءٍ اَحۡصَيۡنٰهُ فِىۡۤ اِمَامٍ مُّبِيۡنٍ٢١۝

Innaa Nahnu nuhyil mawtaa wa naktubu maa qaddamoo wa aasaarahum; wa kulla shai'in ahsainaahu feee Imaamim Mubeen

-Indeed, it is We who bring the dead to life and record what they have put forth and what they left behind, and all things We have enumerated in a clear register.

Tafsir

13

وَاضۡرِبۡ لَهُمۡ مَّثَلًا اَصۡحٰبَ الۡقَرۡيَةِ ​ۘ اِذۡ جَآءَهَا الۡمُرۡسَلُوۡنَۚ‏ ﻿﻿ ٣١۝

Wadrib lahum masalan Ashaabal Qaryatih; iz jaaa'ahal mursaloon

-And present to them an example: the people of the city, when the messengers came to it -

Tafsir

14

اِذۡ اَرۡسَلۡنَاۤ اِلَيۡهِمُ اثۡنَيۡنِ فَكَذَّبُوۡهُمَا فَعَزَّزۡنَا بِثَالِثٍ فَقَالُـوۡۤا اِنَّاۤ اِلَيۡكُمۡ مُّرۡسَلُوۡنَ‏ ٤١۝

Iz arsalnaaa ilaihimusnaini fakazzaboohumaa fa'azzaznaa bisaalisin faqaalooo innaaa ilaikum mursaloon

-When We sent to them two but they denied them, so We strengthened them with a third, and they said, "Indeed, we are messengers to you."

Tafsir

15

قَالُوۡا مَاۤ اَنۡـتُمۡ اِلَّا بَشَرٌ مِّثۡلُـنَا ۙ وَمَاۤ اَنۡزَلَ الرَّحۡمٰنُ مِنۡ شَىۡءٍۙ اِنۡ اَنۡـتُمۡ اِلَّا تَكۡذِبُوۡنَ‏ ٥١۝

Qaaloo maaa antum illaa basharum mislunaa wa maaa anzalar Rahmaanu min shai'in in antum illaa takziboon

-They said, "You are not but human beings like us, and the Most Merciful has not revealed a thing. You are only telling lies."

Tafsir

16

قَالُوۡا رَبُّنَا يَعۡلَمُ اِنَّاۤ اِلَيۡكُمۡ لَمُرۡسَلُوۡنَ‏ ٦١۝

Qaaloo Rabbunaa ya'lamu innaaa ilaikum lamursaloon

-They said, "Our Lord knows that we are messengers to you,

Tafsir

17

وَمَا عَلَيۡنَاۤ اِلَّا الۡبَلٰغُ الۡمُبِيۡنُ‏ ٧١۝

Wa maa 'alainaaa illal balaaghul mubeen

-And we are not responsible except for clear notification."

Tafsir

18

قَالُـوۡۤا اِنَّا تَطَيَّرۡنَا بِكُمۡۚ لَٮِٕنۡ لَّمۡ تَنۡتَهُوۡا لَنَرۡجُمَنَّكُمۡ وَلَيَمَسَّنَّكُمۡ مِّنَّا عَذَابٌ اَلِيۡمٌ‏ ٨١۝

Qaaloo innaa tataiyarnaa bikum la'il-lam tantahoo lanar jumannakum wa la-yamassan nakum minnaa 'azaabun aleem

-They said, "Indeed, we consider you a bad omen. If you do not desist, we will surely stone you, and there will surely touch you, from us, a painful punishment."

Tafsir

19

قَالُوۡا طٰۤٮِٕـرُكُمۡ مَّعَكُمۡؕ اَٮِٕنۡ ذُكِّرۡتُمۡ ؕ بَلۡ اَنۡـتُمۡ قَوۡمٌ مُّسۡرِفُوۡنَ‏ ٩١۝

Qaaloo taaa'irukum ma'akum; a'in zukkirtum; bal antum qawmum musrifoon

-They said, "Your omen is with yourselves. Is it because you were reminded? Rather, you are a transgressing people."

Tafsir

20

وَجَآءَ مِنۡ اَقۡصَا الۡمَدِيۡنَةِ رَجُلٌ يَّسۡعٰى قَالَ يٰقَوۡمِ اتَّبِعُوا الۡمُرۡسَلِيۡنَۙ‏ ٠٢۝

Wa jaaa'a min aqsal madeenati rajuluny yas'aa qaala yaa qawmit tabi'ul mursaleen

-And there came from the farthest end of the city a man, running. He said, "O my people, follow the messengers.

Tafsir

21

اتَّبِعُوۡا مَنۡ لَّا يَسۡـــَٔلُكُمۡ اَجۡرًا وَّهُمۡ مُّهۡتَدُوۡنَ‏ ١٢۝

Ittabi'oo mal-laa yas'alukum ajranw-wa hum muhtadoon

-Follow those who do not ask of you [any] payment, and they are [rightly] guided.

Tafsir

22

وَمَا لِىَ لَاۤ اَعۡبُدُ الَّذِىۡ فَطَرَنِىۡ وَاِلَيۡهِ تُرۡجَعُوۡنَ‏ ٢٢۝

Wa maa liya laaa a'budul lazee fataranee wa ilaihi turja'oon

-And why should I not worship He who created me and to whom you will be returned?

Tafsir

23

ءَاَ تَّخِذُ مِنۡ دُوۡنِهٖۤ اٰلِهَةً اِنۡ يُّرِدۡنِ الرَّحۡمٰنُ بِضُرٍّ لَّا تُغۡنِ عَنِّىۡ شَفَاعَتُهُمۡ شَيۡـــًٔا وَّلَا يُنۡقِذُوۡنِ​ۚ‏ ٣٢۝

'A-attakhizu min dooniheee aalihatan iny-yuridnir Rahmaanu bidurril-laa tughni 'annee shafaa 'atuhum shai 'anw-wa laa yunqizoon

-Should I take other than Him [false] deities [while], if the Most Merciful intends for me some adversity, their intercession will not avail me at all, nor can they save me?

Tafsir

24

اِنِّىۡۤ اِذًا لَّفِىۡ ضَلٰلٍ مُّبِيۡنٍ‏ ٤٢۝

Inneee izal-lafee dalaa-lim-mubeen

-Indeed, I would then be in manifest error.

Tafsir

25

اِنِّىۡۤ اٰمَنۡتُ بِرَبِّكُمۡ فَاسۡمَعُوۡنِؕ‏ ٥٢۝

Inneee aamantu bi Rabbikum fasma'oon

-Indeed, I have believed in your Lord, so listen to me."

Tafsir

26

قِيۡلَ ادۡخُلِ الۡجَـنَّةَ ؕ قَالَ يٰلَيۡتَ قَوۡمِىۡ يَعۡلَمُوۡنَۙ‏ ٦٢۝

Qeelad khulil Jannnah; qaala yaa laita qawmee ya'lamoon

-It was said, "Enter Paradise." He said, "I wish my people could know

Tafsir

27

بِمَا غَفَرَ لِىۡ رَبِّىۡ وَجَعَلَنِىۡ مِنَ الۡمُكۡرَمِيۡنَ‏ ٧٢۝

Bimaa ghafara lee Rabbee wa ja'alanee minal mukrameen

-Of how my Lord has forgiven me and placed me among the honored."

Tafsir

28

۞ وَمَاۤ اَنۡزَلۡنَا عَلٰى قَوۡمِهٖ مِنۡۢ بَعۡدِهٖ مِنۡ جُنۡدٍ مِّنَ السَّمَآءِ وَمَا كُـنَّا مُنۡزِلِيۡنَ‏ ٨٢۝

Wa maaa anzalnaa 'alaa qawmihee mim ba'dihee min jundim minas-samaaa'i wa maa kunnaa munzileen

-And We did not send down upon his people after him any soldiers from the heaven, nor would We have done so.

Tafsir

29

اِنۡ كَانَتۡ اِلَّا صَيۡحَةً وَّاحِدَةً فَاِذَا هُمۡ خٰمِدُوۡنَ‏ ٩٢۝

In kaanat illaa saihatanw waahidatan fa-izaa hum khaamidoon

-It was not but one shout, and immediately they were extinguished.

Tafsir

30

يٰحَسۡرَةً عَلَى الۡعِبَادِ ؔ​ۚ مَا يَاۡتِيۡهِمۡ مِّنۡ رَّسُوۡلٍ اِلَّا كَانُوۡا بِهٖ يَسۡتَهۡزِءُوۡنَ‏ ٠٣۝

Yaa hasratan 'alal 'ibaad; maa yaateehim mir Rasoolin illaa kaanoo bihee yastahzi 'oon

-How regretful for the servants. There did not come to them any messenger except that they used to ridicule him.

Tafsir

31

اَلَمۡ يَرَوۡا كَمۡ اَهۡلَـكۡنَا قَبۡلَهُمۡ مِّنَ الۡقُرُوۡنِ اَنَّهُمۡ اِلَيۡهِمۡ لَا يَرۡجِعُوۡنَؕ‏ ١٣۝

Alam yaraw kam ahlak naa qablahum minal qurooni annahum ilaihim laa yarji'oon

-Have they not considered how many generations We destroyed before them - that they to them will not return?

Tafsir

32

وَاِنۡ كُلٌّ لَّمَّا جَمِيۡعٌ لَّدَيۡنَا مُحۡضَرُوۡنَ٢٣۝

Wa in kullul lammaa jamee'ul-ladainaa muhdaroon

-And indeed, all of them will yet be brought present before Us.

Tafsir

33

وَاٰيَةٌ لَّهُمُ الۡاَرۡضُ الۡمَيۡتَةُ ۖۚ اَحۡيَيۡنٰهَا وَاَخۡرَجۡنَا مِنۡهَا حَبًّا فَمِنۡهُ يَاۡكُلُوۡنَ‏ ٣٣۝

Wa Aayatul lahumul ardul maitatu ahyainaahaa wa akhrajnaa minhaa habban faminhu yaakuloon

-And a sign for them is the dead earth. We have brought it to life and brought forth from it grain, and from it they eat.

Tafsir

34

وَجَعَلۡنَا فِيۡهَا جَنّٰتٍ مِّنۡ نَّخِيۡلٍ وَّاَعۡنَابٍ وَّفَجَّرۡنَا فِيۡهَا مِنَ الۡعُيُوۡنِۙ‏ ٤٣۝

Wa ja'alnaa feehaa jannaatim min nakheelinw wa a'naabinw wa fajjarnaa feeha minal 'uyoon

-And We placed therein gardens of palm trees and grapevines and caused to burst forth therefrom some springs -

Tafsir

35

لِيَاۡكُلُوۡا مِنۡ ثَمَرِهٖ ۙ وَمَا عَمِلَـتۡهُ اَيۡدِيۡهِمۡ​ ؕ اَفَلَا يَشۡكُرُوۡنَ٥٣۝

Liyaakuloo min samarihee wa maa 'amilat-hu aideehim; afalaa yashkuroon

-That they may eat of His fruit. And their hands have not produced it, so will they not be grateful?

Tafsir

36

سُبۡحٰنَ الَّذِىۡ خَلَقَ الۡاَزۡوَاجَ كُلَّهَا مِمَّا تُنۡۢبِتُ الۡاَرۡضُ وَمِنۡ اَنۡفُسِهِمۡ وَمِمَّا لَا يَعۡلَمُوۡنَ‏ ٦٣۝

Subhaanal lazee khalaqal azwaaja kullahaa mimmaa tumbitul ardu wa min anfusihim wa mimmaa laa ya'lamoon

-Exalted is He who created all pairs - from what the earth grows and from themselves and from that which they do not know.

Tafsir

37

وَاٰيَةٌ لَّهُمُ الَّيۡلُ ۖۚ نَسۡلَخُ مِنۡهُ النَّهَارَ فَاِذَا هُمۡ مُّظۡلِمُوۡنَۙ‏ ﻿﻿ ٧٣۝

Wa Aayatul lahumul lailu naslakhu minhun nahaara fa-izaa hum muzlimoon

-And a sign for them is the night. We remove from it [the light of] day, so they are [left] in darkness.

Tafsir

38

وَالشَّمۡسُ تَجۡرِىۡ لِمُسۡتَقَرٍّ لَّهَا ​ؕ ذٰلِكَ تَقۡدِيۡرُ الۡعَزِيۡزِ الۡعَلِيۡمِؕ‏ ٨٣۝

Wash-shamsu tajree limustaqarril lahaa; zaalika taqdeerul 'Azeezil Aleem

-And the sun runs [on course] toward its stopping point. That is the determination of the Exalted in Might, the Knowing.

Tafsir

39

وَالۡقَمَرَ قَدَّرۡنٰهُ مَنَازِلَ حَتّٰى عَادَ كَالۡعُرۡجُوۡنِ الۡقَدِيۡمِ٩٣۝

Walqamara qaddarnaahu manaazila hattaa 'aada kal'ur joonil qadeem

-And the moon - We have determined for it phases, until it returns [appearing] like the old date stalk.

Tafsir

40

لَا الشَّمۡسُ يَنۡۢبَغِىۡ لَهَاۤ اَنۡ تُدۡرِكَ الۡقَمَرَ وَلَا الَّيۡلُ سَابِقُ النَّهَارِ​ؕ وَكُلٌّ فِىۡ فَلَكٍ يَّسۡبَحُوۡنَ٠٤۝

Lash shamsu yambaghee lahaaa an tudrikal qamara wa lal lailu saabiqun nahaar; wa kullun fee falaki yasbahoon

-It is not allowable for the sun to reach the moon, nor does the night overtake the day, but each, in an orbit, is swimming.

Tafsir

41

وَاٰيَةٌ لَّهُمۡ اَنَّا حَمَلۡنَا ذُرِّيَّتَهُمۡ فِى الۡفُلۡكِ الۡمَشۡحُوۡنِۙ‏ ﻿﻿ ٤١۝

Wa Aayatul lahum annaa hamalnaa zurriyatahum fil fulkil mashhoon

-And a sign for them is that We carried their forefathers in a laden ship.

Tafsir

42

وَخَلَقۡنَا لَهُمۡ مِّنۡ مِّثۡلِهٖ مَا يَرۡكَبُوۡنَ٢٤۝

Wa khalaqnaa lahum mim-mislihee maa yarkaboon

-And We created for them from the likes of it that which they ride.

Tafsir

43

وَاِنۡ نَّشَاۡ نُغۡرِقۡهُمۡ فَلَا صَرِيۡخَ لَهُمۡ وَلَا هُمۡ يُنۡقَذُوۡنَۙ‏ ﻿﻿ ٣٤۝

Wa in nashaa nughriqhum falaa sareekha lahum wa laa hum yunqazoon

-And if We should will, We could drown them; then no one responding to a cry would there be for them, nor would they be saved

Tafsir

44

اِلَّا رَحۡمَةً مِّنَّا وَمَتَاعًا اِلٰى حِيۡنٍ٤٤۝

Illaa rahmatam minnaa wa mataa'an ilaa heen

-Except as a mercy from Us and provision for a time.

Tafsir

45

وَاِذَا قِيۡلَ لَهُمُ اتَّقُوۡا مَا بَيۡنَ اَيۡدِيۡكُمۡ وَمَا خَلۡفَكُمۡ لَعَلَّكُمۡ تُرۡحَمُوۡنَ‏ ٥٤۝

Wa izaa qeela lahumuttaqoo maa baina aideekum wa maa khalfakum la'allakum turhamoon

-But when it is said to them, "Beware of what is before you and what is behind you; perhaps you will receive mercy... "

Tafsir

46

وَمَا تَاۡتِيۡهِمۡ مِّنۡ اٰيَةٍ مِّنۡ اٰيٰتِ رَبِّهِمۡ اِلَّا كَانُوۡا عَنۡهَا مُعۡرِضِيۡنَ٦٤۝

Wa maa taateehim min aayatim min ayataati Rabbihim illaa kaanoo 'anhaa mu'rideen

-And no sign comes to them from the signs of their Lord except that they are from it turning away.

Tafsir
